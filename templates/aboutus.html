About Us

Drowsiness Detection System team is a team dedicated to enhancing safety on the roads by detecting and alerting drivers when they show signs of drowsiness.

Mission: Our mission is to prevent accidents caused by drowsy driving by providing an effective and reliable system that monitors driver alertness in real-time.

Vision: At Drowsiness Detection System, we envision a future where road accidents due to drowsy driving are significantly reduced, leading to safer journeys for all.

Values: At the core of our project are values of innovation, safety, and responsibility. We're committed to leveraging technology to save lives and protect communities.
